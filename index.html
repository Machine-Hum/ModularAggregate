Who We Are

We are lowering the cost barrier to modular synths.

Modular Aggregate is a platform designed to help synth creators reach their critical volume where semi-mass manufacturing becomes feasible. Kickstarter is the traditional platform for this, however it's not catered to the modular community, has high fees and is designed for initial funding rounds.

Open Your Shop

Modular Aggregate allows creators to open a store style page with multiple products. Three values are associate with modules: the price, sales volume and expiration time.

Aggregate Sales

Once the sale starts and customers start backing, the number of backers must reach or exceed the number of sales for that module.

Build

The creator now has to deliver the units. As the units were not sold ad-hoc it's possible to proceed with a production run for this sale. Units can be packaged together and tested in bulk. This creates one large job at a known intervals for the creator rather than several smaller time consuming jobs.

Repeat

Modular Aggregate is not a Kickstarting platform. After a successful funding round the cycle will begin again.
